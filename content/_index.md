## About Me

I'm a Data Analyst and Master's student in Data Science at Northwestern University. My experience encompasses supporting multi-site clinical and longitudinal studies in academic medical settings. My work focuses on REDCap database development, data pipeline automation, and ensuring data quality through validation and query systems. I develop data dashboards and operational reports to support study operations and improve data integrity. I primarily use R and SAS, with experience in SQL, Python, and ArcGIS, as well as database design, API integration, and longitudinal data analysis. My work spans clinical trials, medical social science research, and data coordinating center environments.
